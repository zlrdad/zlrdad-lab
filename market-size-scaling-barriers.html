The Trade Agreement Offset

Trade agreements partially compensate for Canada's small domestic market by expanding effective market access:

CPTPP (Trans-Pacific) $12.1T GDP
61% of US market
CETA (EU + Canada) $21.7T GDP
110% of US market
Strategic advantage: Combined CPTPP + CETA access represents ~$33.8T in market opportunity—far exceeding the $19.8T US consumer market.
