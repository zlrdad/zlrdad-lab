What the Outperformers Share

Israel, Sweden, Singapore

✓
Investor-side tax incentives — EIS/SEIS (UK), Angel Tax Credit (Israel), R&D deductions (Sweden)
✓
Government co-investment at scale — Yozma (Israel), Temasek (Singapore), Almi (Sweden)
✓
Founder flywheel effect — Alumni networks creating serial entrepreneurs
✓
Small domestic market → forced global orientation from day one
✓
Talent retention policy — Competitive visas, quality of life, talent clusters

Canada

✗
Investor-side tax incentives — Limited to mining sector; absent for venture/tech
✓
Government co-investment — VCCI, Canada Growth Fund, but modest scale (~$1B)
~
Founder flywheel — Emerging via Shopify, but early stage; need more mega-exits
✓
Global orientation — Large domestic market reduces urgency; retention challenge dominates
✗
Talent retention — Losing top founders & talent to US; visa accessibility lag vs peers
