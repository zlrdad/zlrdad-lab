Key finding: Canada ranks mid-pack or below on nearly every dimension. The compendium reveals a consistent pattern: strong research inputs (GII input rank 13th, 10% of world's top AI researchers) paired with weak commercial conversion (GII output rank 20th, VC/GDP 0.35%, 32% founder retention).
