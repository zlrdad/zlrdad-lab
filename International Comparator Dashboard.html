Canada captures <2% of global AI VC despite 10% of top AI researchers
Canada's financing paradox: Canada deploys C$9.5B in VC but 66% is concentrated in just 26 megadeals. Canada's missing incentive architecture: A Canadian angel investor bears the full $10,000 loss if a startup fails. A UK SEIS investor loses ~$2,750 (72.5% cushion via 50% upfront relief + loss relief). Canada is the only G7 country with no investor-side tax incentive for innovation-sector investment. The existing Flow-Through Share mechanism works well but is restricted to mining and energy.

Founder Retention Patterns

Qualitative comparison of retention mechanisms
Canada's founder exodus: Only 32.4% of Canadian-led high-potential startups launched in 2024 were HQ'd in Canada — down from >67% just five years earlier. Canada's proximity to the US combined with weak retention mechanisms (no angel incentive, paused SUV, no QSBS equivalent) creates a uniquely severe outflow. France's improvement via deliberate brand and policy (La French Tech) suggests the decline is reversible.
Data: Leaders Fund Sep 2025, CB Insights, Dealroom, Brock University, Globe and Mail.
